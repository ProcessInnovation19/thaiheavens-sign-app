## What is ThaiHeavensSignApp?

ThaiHeavensSignApp is a web-based system that allows you to:
- Upload rental contracts (PDF files)
- Create secure signing links for guests
- Allow guests to sign contracts digitally using their finger or mouse
- Download the final signed contracts

**No special software needed** - everything works in your web browser!

## For Administrators

### Getting Started

1. Open the admin page in your web browser
2. You'll see a list of all signing sessions (if any exist)

### Step 1: Upload a PDF Contract

1. Click the **"New Session"** button
2. You'll see an upload area
3. **Option A - Drag and Drop:**
   - Drag your PDF file from your computer
   - Drop it into the upload area
   - The file will upload automatically
4. **Option B - File Picker:**
   - Click the upload area or "Choose File" button
   - Select your PDF file from your computer
   - The file will upload automatically

**Note:** Only PDF files are supported.

### Step 2: Select Signature Position

After uploading, you'll see your PDF displayed:

1. **Navigate Pages:**
   - Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to find the page where the signature should appear

2. **Position the Signature Box:**
   - **Click** anywhere on the PDF where you want the signature to appear
   - A red rectangle will appear showing where the signature will be placed
   - **To move it:** Click and drag the red rectangle to a new position
   - **To resize it:** Click and drag one of the red corner dots
     - The box will maintain its proportions automatically

3. **Fine-tune (Optional):**
   - You can use the **"Calibrate"** tool (available from the main menu) to test signature placement
   - This helps ensure signatures appear exactly where you want them

### Step 3: Create Signing Link

1. After positioning the signature box, click **"Continue"**
2. Fill in the guest information:
   - **Guest Name:** Enter the guest's name (optional but recommended)
   - **Guest Email:** Enter the guest's email (optional)
3. Click **"Create Signing Link"**
4. A signing link will be generated
5. **Copy the link** and send it to your guest via email, text message, or any other method

**Important:** Keep this link secure - anyone with the link can sign the contract!

### Managing Sessions

On the main admin page, you can:

- **View All Sessions:**
  - See all signing sessions you've created
  - View status: Pending, Signed, or Completed

- **Copy Signing Link:**
  - Click the **"Copy URL"** button next to any session
  - The link is copied to your clipboard
  - Send it to your guest

- **Download Signed PDF:**
  - Once a guest has signed and confirmed, a **"Download"** button appears
  - Click it to download the final signed contract

- **Delete Session:**
  - Click the **"Delete"** button to remove a session
  - This permanently deletes the session and associated files
  - **Warning:** This action cannot be undone!

## For Guests (Signing a Contract)

### Receiving the Signing Link

You'll receive a link from the administrator (via email, text, etc.). The link looks like:
```
https://yoursite.com/sign/abc123xyz
```

### Step 1: Open the Link

1. Click the link or paste it into your web browser
2. The contract will load automatically
3. You'll see a welcome message with your name (if provided)

### Step 2: Review the Contract

1. **Read the contract carefully**
2. Use the **"Previous"** and **"Next"** buttons to navigate through all pages
3. Make sure you understand everything before signing

### Step 3: Draw Your Signature

1. Scroll down to the signature area
2. You'll see a white box labeled **"SIGNATURE CANVAS"**
3. **On a computer:**
   - Use your mouse to draw your signature
4. **On a phone or tablet:**
   - Use your finger to draw your signature
5. There's a light gray line to help you align your signature
6. **To start over:** Click the **"Clear Signature"** button

### Step 4: Apply Your Signature

1. Once you're happy with your signature, click **"Apply Signature"**
2. The system will process your signature
3. You'll see a preview of the contract with your signature in place

### Step 5: Review and Confirm

1. **Review the preview:**
   - Check that your signature appears in the correct location
   - Make sure it looks good

2. **If you're satisfied:**
   - Click **"âœ“ Confirm & Download"**
   - Your signature is finalized
   - The signed PDF will download automatically
   - A confirmation message will appear

3. **If you want to change your signature:**
   - Click **"Clear & Sign Again"**
   - You'll return to the signature drawing step
   - Draw a new signature and repeat the process

### After Confirming

- Your signature has been recorded
- The administrator can now download the final signed contract
- You should keep a copy of the downloaded PDF for your records

## Troubleshooting

### "This signing link is not valid or has expired"

**What it means:** The link you're trying to use doesn't exist or has been deleted.

**What to do:**
- Contact the administrator to get a new signing link
- Make sure you copied the entire link correctly

### "Failed to load PDF"

**What it means:** The PDF file couldn't be loaded.

**What to do:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact the administrator if the problem persists

### "Please draw your signature first"

**What it means:** You tried to apply a signature without drawing one.

**What to do:**
- Draw your signature in the signature canvas
- Make sure you can see your signature before clicking "Apply Signature"

### Signature doesn't appear in the preview

**What it means:** There might be an issue with the signature image.

**What to do:**
- Try drawing your signature again
- Make sure you're drawing on the white canvas area
- Click "Clear Signature" and start over

### Can't see the signature box on mobile

**What it means:** The screen might be too small or zoomed in.

**What to do:**
- Zoom out on your mobile browser
- Rotate your device to landscape mode
- Try scrolling to see the signature area

### PDF won't load

**What it means:** There might be an issue with the PDF file or your browser.

**What to do:**
- Try a different web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Make sure your browser is up to date
- Check your internet connection
- Contact the administrator

### Can't download the signed PDF

**What it means:** The download might be blocked by your browser or the file isn't ready.

**What to do:**
- Check your browser's download settings
- Make sure pop-ups aren't blocked
- Try right-clicking the download button and selecting "Save link as..."
- Contact the administrator if the issue persists

## Tips for Best Results

### For Administrators:

1. **Test the signing link yourself** before sending it to guests
2. **Position the signature box carefully** - use the calibration tool if needed

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to install any software?

**A:** No! Everything works in your web browser. No downloads or installations needed.

### Q: Is my signature secure?

**A:** Yes. Your signature is embedded directly into the PDF and cannot be easily removed or altered.

### Q: Can I sign on my phone?

**A:** Yes! The system is designed to work on phones, tablets, and computers.

### Q: What if I make a mistake while signing?

**A:** You can click "Clear Signature" to start over, or "Clear & Sign Again" after applying to redo your signature.

### Q: Can I sign multiple contracts?

**A:** Each signing link is for one contract. If you need to sign multiple contracts, you'll receive separate links.

### Q: How long is the signing link valid?

**A:** The link remains valid until the administrator deletes the session or you complete the signing process.

### Q: Can I print the signed contract?

**A:** Yes! After downloading, you can print the PDF like any other document.

### Q: What file format is the signed contract?

**A:** The signed contract is a standard PDF file that can be opened in any PDF viewer.
